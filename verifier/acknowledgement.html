This project could not have been done without the tremendous help from Lawrenceville Alumni Dr. Lawrence Ma’80 and his team. Unfortunately before the project was finished, Dr Ma passed away in November 2022. I am grateful for all the guidance he gave me during my journey of blockchain. By completing the project alongside with his business partner, I hope to continue his mission of accelerating advancement and adoption of blockchain technology.
